About Us

Founded on a passion for pristine audio, Amplitude has grown from a small boutique in 2000 into a global audio innovator... We blend cutting-edge engineering with user-centric design to bring you sound systems that not only perform but inspire. Our R&D team works tirelessly to integrate high-resolution codecs and advanced DSP, while our quality control ensures every speaker, amplifier, and cable meets rigorous performance and durability standards. From eco-friendly packaging to AI-tuned sound profiles that adapt to your room’s acoustics and your favorite genres, we’re committed to pushing the boundaries of what audio can be. Whether you’re an audiophile chasing every nuance or someone who just loves great music on the go, our goal is the same: to make every listening experience unforgettable.

Company Mission

Our mission is to deliver the highest quality audio equipment with exceptional service and innovation, catering to both audiophiles and casual listeners alike. We invest heavily in R&D to integrate the latest technologies—like high-resolution codecs and advanced DSP—into products you can afford. Every component is hand-tested to meet rigorous performance and durability standards, and we minimize our environmental footprint through sustainable materials and eco-friendly packaging.

History

  • 2000: Amplitude founded in Sound City, SC.
  • 2005: Expanded to include home stereo systems, launching our first 5.1 surround package.
  • 2010: Introduced the BassBlaster series, redefining car audio power and clarity.
  • 2015: Launched our e-commerce platform for global reach.
  • 2018: Partnered with major streaming services to optimize wireless receivers for TIDAL and Spotify.
  • 2020: Adopted eco-friendly packaging and sustainable materials across our product lines.
  • 2023: Rolled out interactive multimedia demos in flagship stores.
  • 2025: Debuted AI-tuned sound profiles that adapt EQ automatically to room acoustics and music genres.

FAQ

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products, provided items are in original condition.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be changed or canceled within 2 hours of placement. Please contact our support team immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Is there a warranty on your products?

Yes, all products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects and malfunctions.

How do I get technical support for my product?

You can contact our technical support team via the Contact Us page or call our hotline during business hours.
